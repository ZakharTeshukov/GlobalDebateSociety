Frequently Asked Questions

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Visits to our offices are by appointment only. Please contact the specific regional office you wish to visit to schedule an appointment at least 3 business days in advance.

For partnership inquiries, please email partnerships@globaldebatesociety.org or use our contact form and select "Partnerships" from the department dropdown. Our partnerships team will get back to you promptly.
