Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Global Debate Society

How do I become a member of Global Debate Society?

You can become a member by filling out the registration form on our Join Us page. We offer three membership tiers: Student, Standard, and Premium, each with different benefits and pricing. After completing the registration and payment process, you'll receive a confirmation email with your membership details.

What are the benefits of membership?

Membership benefits vary by tier but generally include:

  • Access to debate events (local, national, or international depending on your tier)
  • Training resources and materials
  • Community forum access
  • Regular workshops
  • Networking opportunities with fellow debaters
  • Potential eligibility for competitions

You can view the full list of benefits for each membership tier on our Join Us page.

How long does membership last?

All memberships are valid for one year from the date of registration. You'll receive a renewal notice via email approximately one month before your membership expires, giving you the option to continue your membership.

How often do you host debate events?

We host regular events throughout the year:

  • Local debate meetups: Monthly in major cities across Southeast Asia
  • National competitions: Quarterly in rotating host countries
  • International championships: Twice yearly (Spring and Fall)
  • Online debates and workshops: Weekly

Check our Events page for the latest schedule and registration details.

Can non-members attend events?

Some events are open to non-members, while others are exclusive to members. Open events typically require a registration fee, while members may attend for free or at a discounted rate depending on their membership tier. Public workshops and introductory sessions are usually open to all to encourage new people to experience debate.

What training resources do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of training resources:

  • Debate guides and manuals
  • Video tutorials from champion debaters
  • Practice exercises and scenarios
  • Argument construction frameworks
  • Live workshops and coaching sessions
  • Feedback on recorded practice debates
  • Research methodology guides

The level of access to these resources depends on your membership tier. Visit our Resources page to learn more.

I'm a complete beginner. Is Global Debate Society suitable for me?

Absolutely! We welcome debaters of all experience levels. For beginners, we recommend:

  • Starting with our introductory workshops
  • Using our beginner-friendly learning materials
  • Participating in practice debates with peer mentoring
  • Attending local events to observe debates before participating

Many of our most successful members started with no prior debate experience.

How can I participate in competitions?

Competition eligibility depends on your membership tier and experience level:

  • Local competitions: Open to all membership tiers
  • National competitions: Standard and Premium members
  • International competitions: Premium members only

To participate, you'll need to register through our events portal. Some competitive events may have qualification requirements based on previous performance or experience level.

What debate formats do you use in competitions?

We utilize several popular debate formats in our competitions:

  • Asian Parliamentary format
  • British Parliamentary format
  • World Schools format
  • Policy Debate format
  • Lincoln-Douglas format
  • Public Forum format
  • Oxford-style debates for special events

Each competition will specify the format in advance, and training resources are available for all formats.

Are there prizes for winning competitions?

Yes, our competitions typically offer prizes including:

  • Trophies and medals
  • Cash prizes for major championships
  • Scholarships for educational opportunities
  • Sponsored travel to international competitions
  • Recognition through our network of partner organizations

Prize details are announced in the competition briefing materials prior to each event.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, feel free to contact us directly.
