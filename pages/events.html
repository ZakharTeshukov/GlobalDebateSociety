Southeast Asian Debate Championship

Join the premier debate competition in Southeast Asia with teams from across the region. This three-day championship will feature preliminary rounds, elimination debates, and a grand final showcasing the best debaters in the region.

How to Register

Registration for our events is a simple process. Select the event you're interested in, fill out the form with your details, and submit your registration. Our team will review your application and send a confirmation email within 48 hours.

Registration Benefits

  • Early access to event materials
  • Reserved seating (if applicable)
  • Certificate of participation
  • Networking opportunities

For team registrations or special accommodations, please contact us directly at events@globaldebatesociety.org.
