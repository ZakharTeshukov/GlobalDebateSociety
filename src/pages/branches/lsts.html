About Our Branch

The LSTS Debate Club represents the pinnacle of academic discourse at Le Quy Don Technical School for Special Talents. Established in 2019, our club has quickly risen to prominence by combining technical expertise with persuasive argumentation. We focus on developing well-rounded debaters who excel in both scientific reasoning and rhetorical skills.

Quick Remarks

Known for our strong performance in technology and science-focused debate topics, with a special emphasis on STEM integration in competitive debate.
