About Our Branch

The ABCIS Debate Club is a vibrant community of passionate debaters who strive for excellence in public speaking and critical thinking. Established in 2020, we have quickly grown to become one of the most active debate clubs in Ho Chi Minh City, fostering an environment where students can develop their argumentation skills and engage in meaningful discussions about global issues.

Quick Remarks

Known for our strong performance in regional tournaments and commitment to developing novice debaters through our mentorship program.
