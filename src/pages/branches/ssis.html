About Our Branch

The SSIS Debate Club stands as a cornerstone of academic excellence and intellectual discourse at Saigon South International School. Since our founding in 2018, we have cultivated a tradition of rigorous debate, critical analysis, and eloquent argumentation. Our club provides a platform for students to engage with contemporary global issues while developing essential leadership and communication skills.

Quick Remarks

Recognized for our innovative approach to debate training and our successful track record in international competitions.
