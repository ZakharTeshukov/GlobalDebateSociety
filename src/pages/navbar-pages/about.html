About Us

Empowering students through the art of debate and critical thinking.

Discover our mission, values, and the dedicated team working to foster a global community of critical thinkers.

Our Mission

To foster critical thinking, effective communication, and global awareness through debate education and competitions.

The Global Debate Society (GDS) is dedicated to promoting the art and skill of debate among students across Southeast Asia. We believe that debate is not just an academic exercise but a transformative experience that equips young people with essential life skills.

Through our programs, competitions, and resources, we aim to create a vibrant community of debaters who are articulate, thoughtful, and engaged with the pressing issues of our time.

Our Values

Critical Thinking

We champion the development of analytical skills and reasoned judgment.

Effective Communication

We believe in the power of clear, persuasive, and respectful discourse.

Global Perspective

We encourage understanding diverse viewpoints and cultural contexts.

Collaborative Learning

We foster a supportive community where knowledge is freely shared.
